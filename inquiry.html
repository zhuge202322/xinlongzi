RFQ checklist

For faster feedback, include these details in your first message:

  • Product Photo, drawing, model or application scenario.
  • Quantity First order quantity and expected repeat volume.
  • Finish Powder color, chrome, dipping, plating or polishing requirement.
  • Packaging Color box, kraft box, label, barcode and carton mark.
  • Market Destination country and any retailer or platform requirements.
